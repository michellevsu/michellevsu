Music

Music has always been a part of my life, whether through playing instruments, singing, or just listening to music.
  • I learned piano at an early age and still love to play popular melodies for fun.
  • I have also played a Chinese instrument called the erhu for 10 years and enjoyed performing with my Chinese orchestra.
  • Once I got to college, I decided to deepen my interest in singing by joining Trogons A Cappella.

Art&Design

After learning how to use Adobe Creative Cloud software in high school journalism, my interest in art developed into a passion for digital design.
  • I design flyers, digital graphics, logos, and apparel for various student organizations and love seeing the results of my work.
  • Occasionally, I still like to pull out my paintbrush and create art the traditional way.
  • I have also found typography and hand-lettering extremely interesting and I constantly experiment with new styles and marker pens to practice my lettering.

Exploring

Exploring the outdoors, trying new foods, and travelling are some of my favorite things.
  • When I'm back in the Bay Area, I love to hike and bike and take walks around my neighborhood.
  • So far, I have travelled to Taiwan, New Zealand, China, Honduras, Belize, and Mexico. I hope to travel more, especially to France where I can practice my French skills.
